About Us

Big Planetarium is a Bristol-based planetarium that aims to make space exciting, engaging, and accessible for everyone. We use fun and interesting ways to explore science, and we want to inspire curiosity about space while helping people of all ages learn more about the universe around them. We do not want anyone to feel left out of the wonders of space, and we strive to make complicated topics easier to understand.


Our website was designed to introduce people to the Milky Way galaxy and the planets within our solar system in a simple way.

Our Mission

We want to inspire curiosity about space and help people of all ages learn more about the universe around them.

What We Offer

We provide educational entertainment for the public and for schools, using fun and engaging ways to explore science.

Why Space?

Space encourages imagination, discovery, and wonder. It helps us understand both our planet and our place in the universe.
