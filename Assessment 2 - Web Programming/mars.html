Mars

  • Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System.
  • It is often called the "Red Planet" because iron oxide on its surface gives it a reddish colour.
  • Mars is a dusty, cold, desert world with a thin atmosphere.
  • It has seasons, polar ice caps, canyons, and extinct volcanoes.
  • Mars has been explored for decades and is one of the most likely places to find evidence of past or present life beyond Earth.

Quick Facts

Position

The fourth planet from the Sun.

Moons

Mars has two small moons: Phobos and Deimos.

Year Length

687 Earth days.

Fun Fact

Mars is home to the largest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons.
