Explore the Milky Way

The milky way is a galaxy that contains our solar system. It is filled with stars, planets, dust, and gas, and it helps us understand how small earth is in our big universe.



It is a spiral galaxy, and the sun is about 25,000 light years from the center of the galaxy. The milky way is just one of billions of galaxies in the universe, and it contains hundreds of billions of stars.
